Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PLAN SALE CONSULTING ("Plansale", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, stores and protects personal information when you visit our website, contact our team, or engage us for SEO, Google Ads, PPC, Meta ads, website development, landing pages, local SEO, creative or related services.

We aim to collect only the information reasonably needed to respond to inquiries, deliver marketing and website services, improve campaign performance, manage accounts, and maintain normal business operations.

1. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This policy applies to information collected through the Plansale website, contact forms, direct email, discovery calls, project onboarding, client communications, and related digital marketing or website service interactions.

It does not automatically govern third-party websites, advertising networks, payment providers, analytics platforms, social media platforms, CRMs or software tools operated by other companies, even when linked from our website or used as part of campaign delivery.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact details, such as your name, business name, email address, phone number and service inquiry details.
  • Project information, such as business goals, location targets, website details, ad account information, budget range and campaign objectives.
  • Technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring pages and approximate location data derived from analytics tools.
  • Communication records, such as emails, submitted forms, call notes, meeting notes, proposals and project correspondence.
  • Billing or administrative information, where needed to issue invoices, confirm services, maintain records or coordinate ongoing work.

3. How We Use Information

We may use personal information to:

  • respond to service inquiries and schedule discovery calls or consultations;
  • prepare proposals, scopes, campaign recommendations and onboarding materials;
  • deliver SEO, PPC, website development, local SEO, content, CRO, social media or related services;
  • manage client relationships, reporting, support, invoicing and operational administration;
  • monitor website performance, traffic patterns, campaign activity and site usability;
  • improve our services, processes, content and marketing communications; and
  • comply with applicable legal, tax, accounting or regulatory obligations.

4. Cookies, Analytics and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies, pixels, analytics tags and similar technologies to understand user activity, improve website experience, measure campaign effectiveness and support remarketing or advertising operations.

These tools may include website analytics, call tracking, advertising conversion tracking, session measurement, user behavior analysis, or embedded features from third-party platforms. You can usually manage cookie settings through your browser or device settings.

5. When We Share Information

We do not sell personal information as part of our normal business operations. We may share information when reasonably necessary with:

  • service providers that help us operate our website, analytics, CRM, communication, payment, hosting or reporting systems;
  • advertising and marketing platforms used to manage campaigns, audience targeting, conversion tracking or lead attribution;
  • contractors, collaborators or specialists working with us on a project under appropriate confidentiality expectations; and
  • legal, regulatory, tax, insurance or professional advisors where required for legitimate business or legal purposes.

We may also disclose information where disclosure is required by law, legal process, court order, contractual obligation, or where reasonably necessary to protect our rights, users, business operations or public safety.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including responding to inquiries, delivering services, maintaining business records, resolving disputes, enforcing agreements and meeting legal, accounting or tax obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, whether you became a client, the length of the project, and applicable legal or recordkeeping requirements.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, disclosure or alteration. However, no website, email transmission, cloud platform or digital storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

For that reason, while we work to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security and you provide information at your own risk.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us to:

  • request access to personal information we reasonably hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate or outdated information;
  • ask questions about how your information is used; or
  • request deletion of certain information, subject to legal, contractual or operational limits.

We may need to verify identity before responding to certain requests. Some information may need to be retained for legal, accounting, security or legitimate business reasons even after a deletion request.

9. Third-Party Platforms and External Links

Plansale may work with or link to third-party platforms such as website hosts, analytics tools, ad platforms, social media tools, booking or meeting software, CRMs, payment providers and government or grant-related websites. Those third parties operate under their own privacy practices and terms.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through links, embeds, lead forms, campaign landing pages or software tools.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for businesses, organizations and adults seeking marketing, advertising, website or growth support. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children for our own independent purposes through this website.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, services, technology, legal obligations, or website practices. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
