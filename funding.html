RAISE Grant Program

One-time grant for eligible entrepreneurs

The RAISE (Randall Activation, Innovation and Scale-up for Entrepreneurs) Grant provides $10,000 to Indigenous, Black, and other racialized entrepreneurs in Ontario after completing program requirements including training, coaching, and networking sessions.

  • $10,000 non-repayable grant upon program completion
  • Business training and coaching from industry experts
  • Networking opportunities with fellow entrepreneurs
  • Eligible costs: Marketing, digital transformation, equipment, operations

Eligibility Requirements

  • Self-identify as Indigenous, Black, or racialized entrepreneur
  • Business located and operating in Ontario
  • Complete mandatory training and coaching sessions
  • Commit to program participation requirements

LOAN PROGRAM

Canada Small Business Financing Program

Government-backed loans to help small businesses purchase equipment and improve facilities.

Up to $1 Million

Small Business Financing Program (CSBFP)

Government-guaranteed business loans

The Canada Small Business Financing Program makes it easier for small businesses to get loans from financial institutions by sharing the risk with lenders. Use funds to purchase equipment, improve leasehold improvements, or buy real property.

  • Loan amounts: Up to $1 million total ($350k for equipment)
  • Government guarantee: 85% loan guarantee reduces lender risk
  • Flexible terms: Up to 10 years for equipment, 15 for real property
  • Competitive rates: Prime + 3% floating or fixed rate options

Eligible Businesses

  • Gross annual revenues of $10 million or less
  • Operating for profit in Canada
  • Most industries eligible (some exceptions apply)

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about government funding programs

Who is eligible for government grants?

Most Canadian small and medium businesses with fewer than 500 employees are eligible. Specific programs have additional requirements such as revenue limits, industry type, or demographic criteria. We help determine which programs fit your business.

How long does the application process take?

Application timelines vary by program. CDAP typically takes 2-4 weeks from application to approval. RAISE and other grants may take 6-12 weeks. We expedite the process by ensuring all documentation is complete and accurate from the start.

Do I need to repay government grants?

No! Grants like CDAP and RAISE are non-repayable funding. You receive the money to invest in your business without any repayment obligation. Loans like CSBFP and BDC financing do require repayment with interest.

What can I use the funding for?

Funding can be used for website development, e-commerce platforms, digital marketing, software purchases, technology upgrades, equipment, training, and consulting services. Each program has specific eligible expenses we'll help you understand.

What is your success rate?

We maintain a 90% approval rate across all funding programs. Our expertise in preparing strong applications, understanding program requirements, and presenting your business effectively significantly increases your chances of approval.

How much does your service cost?

We offer a free initial consultation to assess your eligibility. Our application support services are competitively priced and often recoverable through the grant funding itself. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific needs.
